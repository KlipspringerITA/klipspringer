In a result that was also fitting of our channel, the audio that we recorded all sounded terrible. So it was completely unusable, however that also exposed us to some new challenges that we would never have imagined.

Skills

Creativity

We are a creative group of individual looking to express our thoughts into content that can be enjoyed by anyone. We expanded our ideas by working through issues together and trying new ideas.

Editing

Our awesome editing skill applies unique style to our videos that will reach mass audience. We gained video editing skills through practicing with Adobe Premiere over the course of many videos.

Recording

With our industry-leading recording techniques, we will be able to bring our live-action content into our gameplays. We learned about the struggles of recording audio and video as well as what to do in the future in order to have better results.

Digital Design

Our thumbnails are simple and easy on the eye, doing so allows us to engage our audience right from the start. We improved our digital design skills through the creation of thumbnails, intros and outros, and creative editing.

Cinematography

Being able to transform actions into a video is not easy, many efforts went into the plot and story. We were able to practice cinematography when filming the live-action content, we had to consider elements such as lighting, content, and composure.

Teamwork

Our communication and teamwork skills contributed to the great amount of our success. We used a groupchat to have team conversations and lunchtime to discuss new ideas. Our teamwork was integral to the completion of this project.
